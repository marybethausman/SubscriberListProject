Colby is an Aussiedoodle (half Australian Shepherd/half Poodle) with a goof-ball personality! She loves anything active, from running every morning with her "mom" to catching frisbees and chasing bubbles. She loves to eat vegetables, including broccoli, peppers and carrots (her lunchtime favorite). Her keen sense of smell can detect a deer on a neighborhood walk from 200 yards away, often resulting in dragging her owners on a flat-out chase to find it. She also loves chasing squirrels and chipmunks, but she's never caught one. When there's a shortage of animals to chase, she's content with a ball. Though she's half poodle, she hates water and will not only dodge a lawn sprinkler, but even jumps in the backseat of the car if the windshield washer sprays. Her Aussie tendencies are strong, as she loves to herd people, including on mountain hikes with her family, where she will run from the front to the back of the pack to make sure her "flock" is present and safe. Aussiedoodles are often called "velcro dogs" because they stick close to their owners, and Colby is no exception. See some of her silly photos in the gallery below.
